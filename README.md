# 🛒 Unlocking Growth at Olist
### An End-to-End Data Analytics Case Study

---

## Why I Built This Project

I wanted to experience what a data analyst actually 
does in a real job — not just learn tools in isolation, 
but go through the complete journey: from a business 
problem to a recommendation that a CEO could act on.

This project is that journey.

---

## The Business Problem

Olist is a Brazilian e-commerce marketplace connecting 
thousands of sellers to millions of customers. They have 
100,000 orders. They have revenue. They have growth.

But is that growth sustainable?

That was my starting question. And the answer 
surprised me.

---

## What I Found

After analyzing 96,478 delivered orders across 
9 datasets spanning 2 years — here is what I 
would tell Olist's CEO:

**1. Your growth is real — but fragile.**
Revenue grew 25x in 13 months. But Average Order 
Value dropped from R$175 to R$160. You are getting 
more customers — but each one is worth less.

**2. You have a retention crisis.**
97% of your customers never come back. 
40% are already lost. You are filling a leaking 
bucket — acquiring new customers while losing 
existing ones silently.

**3. Late deliveries are destroying satisfaction.**
On-time delivery scores 4.29 stars. 
Late delivery scores 2.27 stars.
That is a 47% drop in satisfaction — from one 
operational failure.

**4. Some of your best revenue categories are 
quietly losing customer trust.**
bed_bath_table, computers_accessories, and 
watches_gifts generate millions in revenue — 
but score below 4.0 in satisfaction. 
That revenue is at risk.

**5. 2,418 Sleeping Giants are waiting.**
These customers spent an average of R$1,160 each 
— but haven't returned in over 239 days. 
Reactivating them is the single highest ROI 
opportunity in this dataset.

---

## The Approach

This is not a typical analysis where I loaded 
data and plotted charts. I followed the exact 
workflow a data analyst uses in a real job:

Define    → What metrics matter? What are we solving?

Analyze   → Calculate, join, slice, explore

Visualize → Make the data speak

Interpret → What does this actually mean?

Recommend → What should the business do next?


Every decision in this project has a reason.
Every chart answers a specific business question.
Every finding connects to a recommendation.
